Hello, I am Hilda!

Hilda works at Climate Tech & Services for Renewable Energy company in California.


Her forte lies in being a user experience expert, blending her design proficiency from digital products like apps and web interfaces with a profound connection to environmental considerations. Her robust communication and collaboration skills enable her to bridge the gap between digital realms and real-world environmental impact. She is driven by a genuine passion for understanding user needs, problem-solving, and fostering business growth.


With a diverse background encompassing large-scale enterprises, B2B ventures, and diverse sectors such as energy management systems, e-commerce, and mobile apps/platforms, Hilda has consistently led design initiatives marked by innovation and excellence.

She led the end-to-end design process for Formosa and Delta in renewable energy. Meanwhile, in e-commerce, she led B2B platform access initiatives, improving user journeys at Nike. She also enhanced renewal experiences at Autodesk and led CRM designs at 91app with a dedicated team of three. At Foxconn, she improved Android mobile experiences and led in-house requirement system development.

Beyond her professional pursuits, Hilda finds joy in engaging in handcrafting activities while embracing a minimalist lifestyle.
